## Summary
iLLaDA is an 8B parameter open-weight language model that replaces the standard autoregressive architecture with masked diffusion and bidirectional attention, trained on 12 trillion tokens. The paper demonstrates that non-autoregressive diffusion can be competitive with conventional models while enabling novel training techniques like multi-epoch SFT and confidence-based scoring. Model weights and code are openly available for local deployment.
