## Summary
ReMMD introduces a multilingual benchmark (ReMMDBench) and an agentic verification system (ReMMD-Agent) for detecting multimodal misinformation across 500 samples in five languages with 2,756 images. The system decomposes claims into atomic units, retrieves evidence through a persistent memory bank, and produces structured veracity judgments. It addresses the gap between existing benchmarks and real-world deployment conditions by treating misinformation detection as an evidence-selection problem.
