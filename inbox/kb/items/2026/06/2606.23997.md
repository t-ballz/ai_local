## Summary
ChartWalker introduces a benchmark for cross-chart retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) tasks, featuring a hierarchical knowledge graph construction method for charts and a structure-aware sampling algorithm to generate challenging multi-hop reasoning queries. The work addresses limitations in existing benchmarks by creating semantically coherent questions with controlled difficulty, evaluated across major RAG paradigms with an agentic baseline.
