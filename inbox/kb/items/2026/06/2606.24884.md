## Summary
InSight is a framework for enabling robots to autonomously acquire manipulation skills by decomposing demonstrations into reusable primitive actions using VLMs. It combines automatic primitive segmentation, VLM-guided skill discovery, and compositional reuse to reduce dependence on human teleoperation while enabling continual learning on real hardware.
