## Summary
VeriEvol presents a dataset construction and verification technique for improving multimodal mathematical reasoning in vision-language models. It decouples problem evolution (type-aware difficulty scaling) from verification (hypothesis-testing agent with multiple solution channels), scaling SFT data from 10K to 250K samples and achieving 70.46 on MathVerse Vision-Only. The approach demonstrates that systematic data quality and verification can outperform architectural changes.
