## Summary
DomainShuttle is a text-to-video generation framework that preserves subject identity from reference images while enabling flexible cross-domain transformations. It introduces Domain-MoT (separating video and reference processing with domain-aware attention), Video-Reference DualRoPE (positional encoding for subject-level precision), and Cross-Pair Consistent Loss (training objective for intrinsic subject features), achieving 18.7% improvement in cross-domain scenarios.

Wiki-relevant — novel architecture and training technique for subject-driven video generation.
