## Summary
FlowR2A is a method for autonomous driving planning that learns to map rewards directly to action distributions, enabling multimodal decision-making. This approach allows the model to capture multiple valid driving behaviors for the same scenario, reflecting the inherent ambiguity in real-world driving situations.
