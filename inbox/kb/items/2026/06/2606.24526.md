## Summary
AGORA is a benchmark for evaluating language model agents on realistic workplace document reasoning tasks. It pairs 362 verifiable numeric questions with 9,664 authentic workplace documents across eight professional domains, testing agents ability to locate sparse evidence in large archives within context-window constraints. Frontier models max out at 59.4% accuracy and performance varies substantially across professional domains.

Wiki-relevant — benchmark for agentic document reasoning, tests open-weight model reasoning capabilities.
