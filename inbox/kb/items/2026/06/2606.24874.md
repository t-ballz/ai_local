## Summary
FLUX3D is a scalable image-to-3D framework that generates high-fidelity 3D Gaussian Splatting (3DGS) assets from single images by addressing two key bottlenecks: representation learning (via Diffusion-Aligned Structured Latents) and 2D-3D cross-modal alignment (via sparse-aware diffusion transformers). The method achieves state-of-the-art appearance fidelity through a sparse voxel foundation that preserves fine visual details better than existing approaches.
