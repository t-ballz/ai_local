## Summary
QG-MIL proposes a gated transformer aggregator for Multiple Instance Learning in medical imaging, addressing attention concentration problems in existing attention-based MIL methods. The architecture combines normalization techniques and gating mechanisms to improve stability and generalization across six medical imaging benchmarks with +6.1 macro F1 improvement.
