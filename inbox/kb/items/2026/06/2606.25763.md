## Summary
ShutterMuse uses Qwen3-VL-8B to provide real-time photography guidance during capture—advising photographers on framing refinement and pose adjustments. Introduces CaptureGuide-Bench (973 samples) and CaptureGuide-Dataset (~130K samples).

## Verdict
Minor — domain-specific fine-tuned application, not a reusable technique or open architecture.
