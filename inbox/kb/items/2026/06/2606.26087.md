## Summary
MVTrack4Gen improves 4D video generation (novel-view synthesis from monocular video) by using multi-view point tracking as geometric supervision for diffusion models. The technique leverages correspondence information extracted from attention layers to enforce motion consistency and geometric accuracy across views, enabling camera-conditioned models to generate more coherent dynamic scenes.
