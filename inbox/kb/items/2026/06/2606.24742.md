## Summary
World Value Models for Robotic Manipulation introduces WVM, which combines world models with value estimation to improve robotic policy learning from mixed-quality datasets. The paper proposes using world models—naturally suited for temporal reasoning—rather than static vision-language models to evaluate task progression and data quality. Results demonstrate state-of-the-art performance on a new benchmark (Suboptimal-Value-Bench) with real-world validation.
