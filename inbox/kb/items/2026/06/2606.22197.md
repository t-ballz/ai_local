## Summary
Multi4D introduces a competitive multi-level allocation framework for dynamic 3D Gaussian splatting that resolves the motion consistency vs. visual fidelity tradeoff. The method distributes modeling capacity across static structure, persistent dynamic geometry, and transient appearance levels, using residual-driven optimization to adaptively specialize without pre-assignment, enabling high-fidelity rendering with fewer primitives and state-of-the-art 4D segmentation speed.
